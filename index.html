Word-sense Disambiguation

Word-sense disambiguation (WSD) is an open problem of natural language processing. WSD is identifying which sense of a word (i.e. meaning) is used in a sentence, when the word has multiple meanings. In this project, a Naïve Bayes classifier, a Decision Tree, a SVM and a neural network are trained to identify which of the 6 meanings of the word 'interest' is used in a sentence.
